Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How long does coffee stay fresh?

Properly stored coffee beans stay fresh for 2-4 weeks after roasting. We recommend buying small quantities frequently for optimal freshness.

❓ Can I change my order?

Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. Contact us immediately at info@beanboutique.com with your order number.

❓ Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! Orders of 5+ items receive 10% discount. Contact our sales team for wholesale pricing.

❓ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay for secure transactions.

❓ Is international shipping available?

Yes, we ship to most countries. International shipping costs vary by location. Contact us for a quote.

❓ What if my item arrives damaged?

We'll replace any damaged items free of charge. Please report damage within 48 hours with photos for quick resolution.
