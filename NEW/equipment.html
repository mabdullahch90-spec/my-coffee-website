Equipment Usage Tips

🔧 Getting Started with Espresso

Start with pre-ground espresso coffee for consistency. Invest in a burr grinder to grind fresh beans immediately before brewing.

Key steps: Dose properly (18-20g), distribute evenly, tamp firmly, and extract for 25-30 seconds.

⏱️ Brewing Temperature Matters

Water temperature affects extraction significantly. Most coffees brew best between 195-205°F (90-96°C).

Too hot = over-extraction (bitter), Too cool = under-extraction (sour)

🧹 Maintenance & Cleaning

Clean your equipment regularly to maintain flavor and longevity. Descale your machine monthly if in hard water areas.

Proper maintenance ensures your equipment lasts years and produces consistently excellent coffee.
