Coffee Brewing Guide

Pour Over Brewing

Temperature: 195-205°F (90-96°C)

Ratio: 1:16 (coffee to water)

Time: 3-4 minutes

Best for single-origin coffees. Provides full flavor clarity and control.

French Press

Temperature: 200-205°F (93-96°C)

Ratio: 1:15 (coffee to water)

Time: 4 minutes

Creates full-bodied coffee with bold flavors. Great for exploring blend characteristics.

Espresso

Temperature: 200-205°F (93-96°C)

Pressure: 9 bars

Time: 25-30 seconds extraction

Concentrated shot with rich crema. Perfect for milk-based drinks.
